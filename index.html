Marriages and Divorce in Australia

Map of Marriages by State

Illustratating how marriages will be distributed geographically throughout the states of Australia in 2023. States are labeled according to color, with darker reds denoting more marriages. The dropdown can be used to evaluate monthly changes and show how marriage trends vary by location over the course of the year.

Number of Marriages by Year

Showing yearly trends in marriages across Australian states from 2019 to 2022. The line chart highlights significant shifts, including the drop during the 2020 pandemic, allowing for easy comparison between states and identifying areas of consistent growth or decline.

Butterfly Chart of Marriage Rates

Comparing the total number of marriages and the marriage rate per 1,000 people from 2003 to 2023. The left side shows absolute marriage numbers, while the right visualizes population-adjusted rates, offering insights into whether changes in marriage numbers are driven by population size or behavioral shifts.

Bubble Plot of Divorces

The relationship between the divorce rate and the number of divorces in Australia from 2003 to 2023. Each bubble represents a year, with bubble size indicating the total number of divorces and position reflecting the divorce rate per 1,000 people, providing a clear overview of divorce trends over the years.
